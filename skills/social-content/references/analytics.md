# Analytics & Optimization

## Metrics That Matter

**Awareness:**
- Impressions
- Reach
- Follower growth rate

**Engagement:**
- Engagement rate (engagements / impressions)
- Comments (higher value than likes)
- Shares / reposts
- Saves (Instagram)

**Conversion:**
- Link clicks
- Profile visits
- DMs received
- Leads / conversions attributed

---

## Weekly Tracking Checklist

- [ ] Top 3 performing posts — why did they work?
- [ ] Bottom 3 posts — what can you learn?
- [ ] Follower growth trend
- [ ] Engagement rate trend
- [ ] Best posting times (from data, not assumptions)
- [ ] Content pillar performance comparison

---

## Diagnosing Problems

### If engagement is low:
- Test new hooks (the first line is almost always the issue)
- Post at different times
- Try different formats (carousel vs. text-only vs. video)
- Increase native engagement with others before posting
- Check if content actually matches audience interests

### If reach is declining:
- Remove external links from post body
- Slightly increase posting frequency
- Engage more in comments (dwell time signal)
- Test video / visual content
- Check for recent algorithm changes

### If follower growth is stalling:
- Post more consistently
- Engage in comments on popular posts in your niche
- Collaborate with accounts that have your target audience
- Run a giveaway or challenge (platform-specific)
- Audit your profile — is the value proposition clear?

### If conversion is low (traffic, leads, DMs):
- Strengthen your CTA specificity
- Make the link / next step more obvious in bio
- Add more direct offers (not just value content)
- Use DM triggers: "Comment X and I'll send you..."
- Check that your content attracts the right audience

## Platform Benchmarks (approximate)

| Platform | Good engagement rate | Great engagement rate |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | 2–3% | 5%+ |
| Twitter/X | 0.5–1% | 2%+ |
| Instagram | 1–3% | 5%+ |
| TikTok | 5–9% | 15%+ |
| Facebook | 0.5–1% | 3%+ |

*Engagement rate = (likes + comments + shares) / impressions × 100*

## Content Performance Patterns

**What consistently performs well across platforms:**
- Personal stories with a clear lesson
- Specific numbers and data
- Contrarian takes that invite debate
- Step-by-step how-tos
- Behind-the-scenes moments

**What consistently underperforms:**
- Generic motivational content
- Overly promotional posts
- Content without a clear hook
- Cross-posted content without platform adaptation
- Posts that ask for engagement without providing value

---

## Optimization Loop

1. **Post** → track for 48–72 hours
2. **Identify** top and bottom performers
3. **Extract** pattern from top performers (hook type? format? topic?)
4. **Apply** pattern to next week's content
5. **Repeat** — build a personal playbook from your own data

The goal: within 90 days of consistent posting, you should know your top 3 hook types, best 2 formats, and optimal posting times for your specific audience.
